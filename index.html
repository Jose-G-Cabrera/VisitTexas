What makes texas awesome?

Texas is a brillant state where you can find everything from cowboys boots, rodeos and country music to amazing landscapes and historical sites which makes Texas one of the most unique states in America.

San Antonio's River Walk

The San Antonio River stretches several miles along the heart of the city. Along the side of the river you can find a line of restarants where you can relax and enjoy the nice view, take a walk or if you prefer even take a leisure cruise on a river boat.

The Alamo

The Alamo is one of the most important sites in the history of America. This due to the important role during the Texas Revelution, when a small force barricated themselves on the Alamo, the overwhelming Mexican army defeted the force who was barricated on the Alamo. This lead to cry "Remember the Alamo" which eventually railed the state to overcome the Mexicans.

Big Bend National Park

On the Chihuahuan Desert on West Texas on Bend in the Rio Grande river lies some of the most beautiful and unique sceneries in the State of Texas. This park offers an full range of activity spaces for example, hiking trails, camp grounds and its also home to many species of birds to birdwatch.

Guadalupe Mountains

About 100 miles from east El Paso are located the Guadalupe Mountains, one of the highest Peaks in Texas. This national park is also known for its abundance in Wildlife and for the quality of its trails and the awesome view while hiking.

The Fort Worth Stockyards

The historic stockyards located in the heart of Forth Worth Texas. Fundaded in 1866 the area took recognition due to the cattle industry and it was here in this area where a lot of the cattle was shipped to other parts of the state. Now a days the historic stockyard remains as the last surviving facility of its kind and has been transoformed into many public atractions including rodeos, performances and western themed shopping.
